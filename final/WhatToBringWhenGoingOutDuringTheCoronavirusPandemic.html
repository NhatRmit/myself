What To Bring When Going Out During The Coronavirus Pandemic

Project: Propaganda Poster (self-project)
Tool: Adobe Illustrator
Description:
‘WHAT TO BRING WHEN GOING OUT DURING THE CORONAVIRUS PANDEMIC’ is three essential things that Governments encourage citizens to need to bring when they go out. This project was inspired; it also reminds me. Moreover, I hope my poster can convey the message to all people who see this. Stay safe! It is my self-project when I first used Adobe Illustrator. After doing this, I learned the principle of design and color combination. Although I know this project is not complex, I still enjoy seeing this a couple of times because of my knowledge and the message.
